KGS SCHOOL

ADMISSION FORM

KGS SCHOOL is one of Pakistan's oldest and most selective private schools, established in 1847. It operates across three sites: the original Middle Section in Saddar, which is a heritage building for years VII-IX, and two separate Clifton campuses for the Kindergarten/Junior Sections (Nursery-VI) and the College Section (O- and A-Level students). As an independent English-medium school, it prepares students for university entrance exams and offers a range of extracurricular activities.
